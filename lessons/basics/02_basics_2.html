Basic grammar 1 - Podstawowa gramatyka 1

Questions - Pytania

To make a sentence a question You can add Czy at the beginning, or just add a question mark at the end.

Czy jesteś głodna? Are you hungry (♀)?
Masz dzieci? Do you have children?

Czy means whether, or, or if. It can be used to start questions for other verbs.

Grammatical cases - Odmiana przypadek

If you want to search for the cases of a word in a dictionary, you should look to the table labeled: Odmiana przypadek. For now let’s review the rules for the new words from the lesson.

Instrumental Case - Narzędnik

https://mowicpopolsku.com/polish-grammar/cases/instrumental/ Something is defined as being something else. Instrumental male, if it ends in g or k, i is added before the instrumental em k → kiem g → giem

Chłopakiem: Jesteś chłopakiem You are a (teenage) boy.

Instrumental female, same rules as before a → ą

Dziewczyna: Jestem dziewczyną I am a (teenage) girl.

Accusative Case - Biernik

https://mowicpopolsku.com/polish-grammar/cases/accusative/ The direct object - what is being affected by the verb? Accusative neuter (plural), so it doesn't change.

Ciasteczka: One mają ciasteczka (♀)They have cookies.
