Adjectives - Przymiotniki

This is the lesson for food, so we'll just see one adjective: Smaczny - Tasty. Adjectives in polish have to follow declension depending on the gender. For now, we just need to know the nominative (mianownik) form of tasty. For more detail you can see the later lesson on adjectives or go to the following link: https://mowicpopolsku.com/polish-grammar/word-classes/adjectives/#declension

Grammar practice - Ćwiczenia gramatyczne

Plain Adjectives: Smaczna kanapka - Tasty sandwich. Smaczny sok - Tasty juice Smaczne wino - Tasty wine Nominative (Być): Zupa jest smaczna - The soup is tasty. Śniadanie jest smaczne - Breakfast is tasty. Accusative (Biernik): Jem śniadanie i kolację - I eat breakfast and dinner. Jem obiad - I am eating lunch. Jecie posiłek - You (plural) are eating a meal. Mężczyzna ma pomidora * - The man has a tomato * The accusative (biernik) of pomidor would be just pomidor according to the rules, but as an exception to the rule, its declension is pomidora. There are many exceptions, a list of some is here: https://mowicpopolsku.com/polish-grammar/cases/accusative/
